Hygiene & Safety

Clean work. Safe studio. Non-negotiable.

Our Standards

InkFlow Tattoo Studio operates with hospital-grade sterilisation protocols. Every needle is single-use. Every surface is disinfected between sessions.

We use premium-grade inks from trusted brands. Our artists are trained in bloodborne pathogen safety and maintain current hygiene certifications.

Your safety isn't a selling point for us — it's the baseline. We wouldn't tattoo our own friends and family any other way.

What We Do Every Session

Single-Use Needles

Every needle and grip is opened fresh, used once, and disposed of immediately after your session.

Autoclave Sterilisation

All reusable equipment goes through medical-grade autoclave sterilisation before every use.

Premium Inks

Professional-grade inks only. No shortcuts, no unknown brands, no compromises on your skin.
