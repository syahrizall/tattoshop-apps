Booking Policy

How to Book

Send us a message on WhatsApp, Instagram, or Facebook with your tattoo idea. We will advise on design, sizing, placement, and artist match before confirming your session.

Walk-Ins

Walk-ins are welcome daily 10am–8pm, subject to artist availability. For guaranteed slots, book in advance.

Cancellations

Please notify us at least 24 hours before your appointment. Repeated no-shows may require a deposit for future bookings.
